RUML-rg

The Rice Undergraduate Machine Learning Reading Group (RUML-rg) is a loose association of undergraduates interested in discovering more about modern methods in machine learning and artificial intelligence, outside of the regular Rice curriculum. The reading list below displays previously read papers, the papers to be read for the next meeting, and all planned papers (based on the list here). Check the facebook page for the date and location of our next meeting!
